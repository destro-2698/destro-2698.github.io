About Me

My Name is Avi Singh Raghuwanshi, a fourth-year undergraduate student of the Department of Mining Engineering enrolled in its dual degree course. Originating from Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, I am currently a boarder of Lal Bahadur Shastri Hall of Residence.

It has been three years since I first set foot on this beautiful campus of IIT Kharagpur. From the very beginning, I have tried to mold myself to become a harmonious part of this perfect symphony by being involved in activities and initiatives both at institute and hall levels. Working with my fellow KGPians and looking at the plethora of talents and skills we together possess, has helped me believe that by working strategically with the right intentions and a well-coordinated team, we can achieve anything.

People are indeed the greatest asset of any society I have witnessed and experienced the acts of betterment for the institute performed previously and wish for nothing more but to contribute my part to the cause by joining hands with every KGPian. Owing to this I have decided to embark on a quest to give back and serve this prestigious institute by - contesting for the post of Vice President, Technology Students’ Gymkhana, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur for the academic session 2020-2021.
